Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers to help you understand our farm better.

How fresh is the milk when delivered?

Our milk is delivered to your doorstep within 12 to 24 hours of milking. We maintain a strict cold chain to ensure it arrives as fresh as possible.

Is your milk organic and hormone-free?

Yes! Our cows graze on 100% organic pastures. We never use artificial growth hormones (rBST) or preventative antibiotics on our herd.

Is there a home delivery charge?

Yes, we charge a flat $2.00 delivery fee per order, no matter how many items you purchase. This helps us maintain our refrigerated delivery fleet and ensure your milk arrives perfectly cold!

What is the difference between your Raw and Pasteurized milk?

Our Raw Milk is unheated and retains all natural enzymes and good bacteria. Our Pasteurized Milk is gently heated to kill potential pathogens while preserving as much nutritional value and flavor as possible.
